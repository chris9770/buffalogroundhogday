Buffalo Groundhog Day

January 20th, 2020

In 2013 Buffalo Groundhog Day Society Inc. was formed and certified as a not for profit agency in the State of New York. The organization works to raise money for local animals. Having donated over $10,000 to local animal organizations such as the Buffalo Zoo, Buffalo C.A.R.E.S animal shelter and Hawk Creek Wildlife Center, the Buffalo Groundhog Day Society continues to show their dedication to the furry citizens of Western NY. The goal is to provide Buffalo residents with an amazing winter festival, while also working to help local animals in need. Buffalo Groundhog Day Society has 501(c)3 status from the IRS, meaning all donations to our organization are tax deductible.
